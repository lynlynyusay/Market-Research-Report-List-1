<p><p>The Airline Reservation System market is divided into two main types: On-Premise and Web-Based systems. On-Premise solutions are installed locally on airline servers, offering high control and customization but requiring significant infrastructure and maintenance. In contrast, Web-Based systems operate via the internet, enabling easier access, scalability, and lower upfront costs. These systems allow airlines to manage reservations from any location, appealing to businesses seeking flexibility and a reduced need for in-house technical support.</p></p>
