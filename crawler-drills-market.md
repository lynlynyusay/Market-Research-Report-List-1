<p><p>Crawler drills are categorized mainly into hydraulic and pneumatic types, each serving distinct operational environments. Hydraulic crawler drills utilize pressurized fluid to power their drilling mechanisms, offering high efficiency and precision, making them suitable for tough terrains and heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic crawler drills, on the other hand, rely on compressed air for their operations, excelling in softer ground conditions and offering portability. The "Others" category includes alternative technologies and specialized drills designed for niche applications, expanding the versatility of crawler drill solutions.</p></p>
