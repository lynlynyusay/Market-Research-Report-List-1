<p><strong>The IoT Motion Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared (PIR)</li><li>Microwave</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Tomography</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT motion sensor market encompasses various technologies designed to detect movement. Passive Infrared (PIR) sensors detect infrared radiation from objects, primarily humans, making them ideal for energy-efficient applications. Microwave sensors emit microwave signals to track movement, offering greater detection range. Ultrasound sensors utilize sound waves to detect motion, providing precision in close quarters. Tomography sensors create an image of the monitored area through multiple source signals, ensuring robust tracking. Other types include laser-based and hybrid sensors, catering to specific needs.</p></p>
