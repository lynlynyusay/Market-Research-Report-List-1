Additionally, the rise of outpatient care facilities and home healthcare services is contributing to an increased demand for portable and easy-to-use IV cannula systems. Technological innovations, such as smart IV cannulas equipped with sensors, are also emerging, aimed at improving patient safety and monitoring.
