<p><p>Dive hoods are categorized into warm water and cold water markets based on the environmental conditions where diving occurs. Warm water dive hoods are typically made from thinner materials, providing minimal thermal insulation while offering sun protection and comfort in tropical waters. In contrast, cold water dive hoods are constructed from thicker, insulating materials to retain body heat during colder dives, often featuring additional features like sealed seams and extra thermal linings for enhanced warmth and protection against the elements.</p></p>
