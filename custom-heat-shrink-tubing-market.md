<p><p>The custom heat shrink tubing market includes several types tailored for specific applications. ATUM (Aerospace Tubing for Ultraviolet Management) heat shrink tubes are designed for aerospace use, providing UV resistance. BRST (Broad Range Shrink Tubes) are versatile for various industries. BSTS/BSTS-FR tubes offer flame retardant properties, ensuring safety in electrical environments. CGAT (Chemical Grade Adhesive Tubing) heat shrink tubes are engineered to withstand harsh chemical exposures, making them suitable for industrial applications requiring robust protection and durability.</p></p>
