<p><ul><li>Volatile</li><li>Non-Volatile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone elastomer gels in the cosmetics market are categorized into volatile and non-volatile types. Volatile silicone elastomers evaporate quickly upon application, providing a lightweight feel and facilitating fast drying, ideal for formulations like primers and setting sprays. In contrast, non-volatile silicone elastomers remain on the skin, offering long-lasting hydration, improved texture, and a silky finish. Both types enhance product performance, allowing for diverse applications in skincare, makeup, and hair care formulations, catering to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
