<p><p>Foot-Launched Powered Hang Gliders (FLPHGs) are lightweight aircraft that allow pilots to take off and land on foot, using a propulsion system for powered flight. The market is divided into two primary types: prone and supine. Prone FLPHGs position pilots face down, offering a streamlined aerodynamic profile and enhanced speed, making them ideal for performance enthusiasts. In contrast, supine models allow pilots to lie on their backs, providing comfort and visibility, appealing to recreational flyers and those seeking a more relaxed flying experience.</p></p>
