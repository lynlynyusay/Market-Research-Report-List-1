<p><p>A Differential Pressure Liquid Level Transmitter is an instrument used to measure the level of liquids in various industrial applications by utilizing the principle of differential pressure. It works by determining the pressure difference between two points, typically at the bottom of a tank and the atmospheric pressure above the liquid.
