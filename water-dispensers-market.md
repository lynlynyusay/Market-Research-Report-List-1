<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water dispensers market comprises two main types: Bottled Water Dispenser (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) systems. BWC utilizes pre-filled water bottles, providing versatility and easy installation, making it popular for homes and offices. In contrast, POU systems connect directly to the water supply, offering continuous hydration with filtration, reducing environmental impact from plastic waste. Each type caters to different user preferences, sustainability goals, and cost considerations, shaping their distinct roles in the water delivery market.</p></p>
