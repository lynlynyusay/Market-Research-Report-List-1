<p><p>The electric capacitors market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Ceramic capacitors are widely used for their stability and reliability in high-frequency circuits. Film/paper capacitors offer excellent insulation and are common in audio equipment. Aluminium capacitors are popular for their high capacitance in power applications. Tantalum and niobium capacitors provide compact size and high efficiency, often seen in portable electronics. Double-layer or supercapacitors deliver rapid charge and discharge cycles, ideal for energy storage systems and regenerative braking in electric vehicles.</p></p>
