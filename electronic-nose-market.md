<p><p>The electronic nose market is primarily categorized into four types based on sensing technology: Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS), Conductive Polymer (CP), Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), and others. MOS sensors utilize the change in resistance to detect gases, while CP sensors change conductivity in response to volatile compounds. QCM technology measures frequency shifts as gas molecules adsorb onto the sensor surface. The "Others" category includes various alternative technologies, enhancing the market's versatility across applications like food quality, environmental monitoring, and health diagnostics.</p></p>
