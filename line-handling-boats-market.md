<p><p>Line-Handling Boats are essential for managing lines and mooring operations in various maritime environments. The market for these boats is primarily divided into two categories: Inboard Motor and Outboard Motor boats. Inboard Motor boats feature engines housed within the vessel, offering stability and space, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, Outboard Motor boats have engines mounted externally, providing greater maneuverability and speed, making them suitable for quick line-handling operations. Both types cater to specific operational needs and preferences in the maritime industry.</p></p>
