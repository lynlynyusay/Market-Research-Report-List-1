<p><p>The hydrostatic level sensors market is primarily divided into two types: switch type and continuous type. Switch type sensors detect specific liquid levels, activating at predetermined thresholds, making them suitable for applications like overflow prevention. Continuous type sensors, on the other hand, provide real-time level measurements across a range, allowing for more precise monitoring of liquid levels in processes like water management and industrial applications. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring efficient operation and safety in various industries.</p></p>
