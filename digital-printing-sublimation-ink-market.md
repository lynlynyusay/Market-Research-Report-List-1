<p><strong>The Digital Printing Sublimation Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Sublimation</li><li>Transfer Sublimation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital printing sublimation ink market is primarily divided into two types: direct sublimation and transfer sublimation. Direct sublimation involves printing directly onto polyester fabrics, allowing for vibrant colors and durable results. This method is commonly used in apparel and soft signage. Conversely, transfer sublimation prints the design on a special transfer paper, which is then heat-pressed onto the substrate. This technique is versatile and suitable for various materials, including ceramics and metals, enhancing creative possibilities in printing.</p></p>
