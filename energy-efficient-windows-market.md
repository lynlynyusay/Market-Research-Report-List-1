<p><p>The energy-efficient windows market includes various types designed to enhance thermal performance. Double glazing consists of two glass panes separated by an insulating gas layer, reducing heat transfer. Triple glazing adds an additional pane, further minimizing energy loss. Low-E (low emissivity) coatings can be applied to either type, reflecting heat back indoors while allowing sunlight in, improving energy efficiency. These windows contribute to lower utility bills and increased comfort in homes, driving demand for sustainable building solutions.</p></p>
