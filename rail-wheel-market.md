<p><p>The rail wheel market encompasses several types, primarily categorized into rolled rail wheels and forged rail wheels. Rolled rail wheels are produced through processes involving rolling metal, offering cost-effectiveness and uniformity, making them suitable for light to medium applications. In contrast, forged rail wheels are manufactured by shaping heated metal under pressure, providing superior strength and durability, ideal for heavy and high-performance rail vehicles. Other types may include composite or specialized rail wheels designed for unique operational requirements or environments.</p></p>
