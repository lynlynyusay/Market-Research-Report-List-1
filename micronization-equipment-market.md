<p><p>The micrоnization equipment market includes various technologies for reducing particle size. Airflow mills utilize high-velocity air to achieve fine particle dispersion, suitable for heat-sensitive materials. Ball mills employ the impact and friction of balls to grind substances into micron-sized particles, effective for a broad range of materials. Vibrating mills use high-frequency vibrations to facilitate size reduction, offering efficient grinding and blending capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and materials processing.</p></p>
