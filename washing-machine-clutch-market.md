<p><ul><li>Wave Type</li><li>Drum Type</li><li>Stirring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The washing machine clutch market comprises three primary types: wave type, drum type, and stirring type. Wave type clutches use a flexible, wave-shaped design to engage and disengage the machine's components smoothly. Drum type clutches utilize a cylindrical mechanism that provides reliable torque transmission, ideal for heavy loads. Stirring type clutches integrate a stirring motion to enhance washing efficiency, ensuring thorough agitation of clothes. Each type offers distinct advantages for varying washing machine designs and performance requirements.</p></p>
