<p><p>The Lead Shielded Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) market features two primary types: Type A2 and Type B2. Type A2 cabinets are designed for biological containment and allow for recirculation of air within the cabinet, ideal for low-risk materials. Type B2 cabinets, on the other hand, provide a higher level of safety with their laboratory exhaust systems, ensuring that all air is vented outside, making them suitable for handling hazardous agents. These distinctions cater to varying safety requirements in laboratory environments.</p></p>
