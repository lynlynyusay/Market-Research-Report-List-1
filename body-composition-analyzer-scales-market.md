<p><strong>The Body Composition Analyzer Scales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bio-Impedance Analyzers</li><li>Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry</li><li>Air Displacement Plethysmography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Body composition analyzer scales utilize various technologies to assess body composition metrics. Bio-Impedance Analyzers measure the resistance of body tissues to electrical currents, providing estimates of fat and lean mass. Dual-Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) uses low-level X-rays to differentiate body tissues, giving precise measurements of bone density and fat. Air Displacement Plethysmography uses air pressure to gauge body volume, offering accurate body density insights. Other methods may include skinfold measurements or near-infrared interactance, expanding the options for body composition assessment.</p></p>
