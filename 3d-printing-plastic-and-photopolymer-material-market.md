<p><p>The 3D printing plastic and photopolymer material market encompasses several key technologies. Fused Deposition Modelling (FDM) uses thermoplastic filaments, heating and extruding them layer by layer. Stereolithography (SLA) employs UV light to cure liquid photopolymer into solid forms. Polyjet Printing jets photopolymer droplets and cures them, allowing for multi-material production. Laser Sintering fuses powdered materials using a laser for durable parts. Digital Light Processing (DLP) uses a projector to cure resin rapidly. Each technology serves diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
