<p><strong>The Fibre Optics Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intensity Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Phase Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Wavelength Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li><li>Polarization Modulated Fibre Optics Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optics sensors market includes four primary types. Intensity modulated fiber optics sensors measure light intensity changes to detect variations in physical parameters. Phase modulated sensors rely on shifts in the phase of light waves, offering high sensitivity for strain measurement. Wavelength modulated sensors detect shifts in light wavelength to monitor temperature and pressure changes. Polarization modulated sensors analyze alterations in the polarization state of light, making them suitable for detecting external disturbances in environments.</p></p>
