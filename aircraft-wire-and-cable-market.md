<p><p>The aircraft wire and cable market is divided into two primary types: aircraft wire and aircraft cable. Aircraft wire typically refers to insulated conductors designed for electrical systems, featuring high heat and flame resistance to ensure safety and reliability in aviation applications. Aircraft cable, on the other hand, encompasses multi-conductor assemblies used for transmitting signals and power, often incorporating shielded designs to minimize interference. Both types are essential for ensuring the performance and safety of modern aerospace systems.</p></p>
