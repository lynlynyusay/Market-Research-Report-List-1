<p><p>The polyurethane spray machine market is segmented into air-powered, electric, and hydraulic types, each catering to different application needs. Air-powered machines utilize compressed air to atomize the polyurethane, offering portability and ease of use. Electric machines provide consistent power and are suitable for smaller projects requiring precision. Hydraulic machines deliver high pressure and are ideal for large-scale applications, ensuring efficient material usage. Each type serves various industries, including construction, automotive, and furniture, enhancing operational efficiency and quality.</p></p>
