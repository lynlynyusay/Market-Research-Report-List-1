<p><strong>The Nickel Metal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nickeliferous Limonite Smelting</li><li>Garnierite Smelting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nickel metal market includes various types based on ore processing methods. Nickeliferous limonite smelting involves extracting nickel from low-grade, iron-rich laterite ores, typically using pyrometallurgical processes like blast or electric furnaces. Garnierite smelting focuses on higher-grade nickel silicate ores, often processed through hydrometallurgical methods like high-pressure acid leaching. Both markets cater to stainless steel and battery industries, with limonite-derived nickel being cost-effective for bulk applications and garnierite-derived nickel offering higher purity for specialized uses like electric vehicle batteries.</p></p>
