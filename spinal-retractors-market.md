<p><ul><li>Blade Retractor</li><li>Tubular Retractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal retractors market is categorized mainly into two types: blade retractors and tubular retractors. Blade retractors are designed to provide broad access to the surgical site by holding back tissues, typically used in open surgeries. Tubular retractors, on the other hand, feature a narrow, tubular shape that minimizes tissue disruption and is ideal for minimally invasive procedures. Both types enhance visibility and access during spinal surgeries, improving surgical precision and patient outcomes.</p></p>
