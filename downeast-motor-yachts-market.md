<p><p>Downeast Motor Yachts are categorized into three main hull types: planing, displacement, and semi-displacement. Planing hulls are designed for high speeds, skimming over the water's surface, appealing to those seeking excitement and performance. Displacement hulls, on the other hand, travel through the water and are known for their efficiency, stability, and comfort, catering to long-distance cruising. Semi-displacement hulls combine features of both, allowing for moderate speed while maintaining some efficiency, making them versatile for various boating experiences.</p></p>
