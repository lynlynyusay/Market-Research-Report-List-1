<p><p>Emulsion Polymerization Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (E-SBR) is a synthetic rubber widely used in tire manufacturing and various industrial applications. The market is divided into two main types: oil-extended and non-oil extended. Oil-extended E-SBR contains processing oils to enhance flexibility and reduce production costs, making it suitable for applications requiring elasticity. In contrast, non-oil extended E-SBR lacks these oils, offering superior strength and abrasion resistance, which is ideal for high-performance applications like automotive tires and industrial products.</p></p>
