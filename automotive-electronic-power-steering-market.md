<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electronic power steering (EPS) market includes three main types: C-EPS, P-EPS, and R-EPS. C-EPS, or column-electric power steering, integrates the motor within the steering column, offering compact design and weight savings. P-EPS, or pinion-electric power steering, positions the motor at the pinion gear, enhancing performance and responsiveness. R-EPS, or rack-electric power steering, directly drives the steering rack, providing precise control and improved feedback. Each type contributes to vehicle efficiency, safety, and handling dynamics.</p></p>
