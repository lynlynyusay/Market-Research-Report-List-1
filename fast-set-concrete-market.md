<p><p>The Fast Set Concrete Market is divided into two main types: Chemical Additive Concrete and Mineral Additive Concrete. In contrast, Mineral Additive Concrete relies on natural or processed minerals, such as fly ash or silica fume, to modify properties like durability and workability.
