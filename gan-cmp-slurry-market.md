<p><ul><li>Colloidal Silica Polishing Slurry</li><li>Alumina Polishing Slurry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN CMP slurry market primarily consists of two types: colloidal silica polishing slurry and alumina polishing slurry. Colloidal silica slurry is favored for its ability to provide superior surface finish and low defect density, making it ideal for precise polishing of GaN substrates. In contrast, alumina polishing slurry offers effective material removal rates and is suitable for coarse polishing applications. Both types play crucial roles in achieving the required flatness and smoothness for GaN semiconductor devices.</p></p>
