<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Direct Fired Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiant Heater</li><li>Convection Heater</li><li>Radiant-Convection Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct fired heater market comprises three main types: radiant heaters, convection heaters, and radiant-convection heaters. Radiant heaters emit infrared radiation to directly warm objects and surfaces, making them effective for spot heating. Convection heaters, on the other hand, circulate heated air to warm larger areas, providing more uniform temperature distribution. Radiant-convection heaters combine both methods, offering efficient heating through radiative warmth and warmed air circulation, making them versatile solutions for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
