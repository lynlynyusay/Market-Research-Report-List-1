<p><p>The Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) Devices market comprises several key products used in treating coronary artery disease. Coronary stents are small mesh tubes placed in arteries to keep them open. PTCA catheters are used for balloon angioplasty, widening narrowed arteries. Coronary guidewires facilitate the navigation of devices within blood vessels. Embolic protection devices capture or filter debris released during procedures, preventing complications. Together, these devices enhance the safety and effectiveness of PCI procedures in improving cardiovascular health.</p></p>
