<p><p>The 4C superfast charging battery market features two prominent types: Ternary Lithium Batteries and Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) Batteries. Ternary Lithium Batteries, known for their high energy density and efficiency, utilize nickel, cobalt, and manganese, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid charging and high performance, such as electric vehicles. In contrast, Lithium Iron Phosphate Batteries offer enhanced safety and longevity, with a lower energy density, making them ideal for stationary energy storage and applications where thermal stability is crucial.</p></p>
