<p><strong>The Infectious Disease Testing Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Diagnostic Test</li><li>POCT</li><li>Immunodiagnostic Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Infectious disease testing kits comprise three main market types: </p><p>1. **Molecular Diagnostic Tests** utilize techniques like PCR to detect pathogens' genetic material, offering high specificity and sensitivity. </p><p>2. **Point-of-Care Testing (POCT)** provides rapid results directly at the patient's location, improving accessibility and timely decision-making. </p><p>3. **Immunodiagnostic Tests** detect antibodies or antigens related to infections, often using methods like ELISA, facilitating the identification of current or past infections. Collectively, these tests enhance disease management and control.</p></p>
