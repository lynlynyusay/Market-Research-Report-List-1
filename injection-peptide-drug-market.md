<p><p>The Injection Peptide Drug Market is primarily segmented into two types: intravenous (IV) injection and subcutaneous (SC) injection. IV injection delivers peptides directly into the bloodstream, ensuring rapid onset and immediate therapeutic effect, making it suitable for emergencies or conditions requiring quick intervention. Conversely, SC injection administers peptides into the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, offering ease of use, comfort, and slower absorption rates, making it ideal for chronic conditions requiring long-term treatment and self-administration.</p></p>
