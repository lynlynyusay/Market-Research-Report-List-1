<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cooking oil testers are essential tools for assessing the quality and safety of cooking oil. The market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless testers. Contact testers involve direct interaction with the oil, using probes or sensors to measure properties like free fatty acids and oxidation levels. In contrast, contactless testers utilize infrared or other non-invasive technologies to analyze oil quality without any physical contact, providing a faster and often more hygienic method for monitoring cooking oil.</p></p>
