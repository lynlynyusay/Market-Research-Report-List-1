<p><ul><li>CNC Bench Drill</li><li>Non-NC Bench Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bench drill market comprises two main types: CNC bench drills and non-NC bench drills. CNC bench drills utilize computer numerical control technology for precision drilling, offering automation and the ability to perform complex tasks with minimal operator intervention. In contrast, non-NC bench drills rely on manual controls, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness for basic drilling needs. Both types cater to different user requirements, with CNC models favored in industrial settings and non-NC models preferred for smaller workshops or home use.</p></p>
