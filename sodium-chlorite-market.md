<p><p>The sodium chlorite market is segmented into two primary types: liquid sodium chlorite and solid sodium chlorite. Liquid sodium chlorite is commonly used in water treatment, disinfection, and bleaching applications due to its effectiveness as a biocide and oxidizing agent. Solid sodium chlorite, on the other hand, is often utilized in various industrial processes and as a precursor for generating chlorine dioxide in bleaching and sanitization. Both types serve crucial roles across multiple sectors, including healthcare, agriculture, and food processing.</p></p>
