<p><p>The video server market is characterized by various technology types, each catering to different streaming needs. M-JPEG technology compresses video using individual JPEG images, offering simple implementation but lower efficiency. MPEG-4 enhances compression and quality, allowing for streaming over limited bandwidth, ideal for web applications. H.264 technology provides superior compression, delivering high-quality video at lower bitrates, making it widely used in online streaming and broadcasting. Each technology balances quality, bandwidth efficiency, and application suitability within the market.</p></p>
