<p><p>The folded filter element market comprises various types designed for specific applications and filtration needs. Polypropylene (PP) folding filter elements offer chemical resistance and are ideal for industrial processes. PES folded filter elements provide high-temperature stability and low protein binding, suitable for biopharmaceuticals. Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) folding filters excel in chemical resistance and high flow rates, while Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) elements feature durability and UV resistance. Nylon Film (N66) folded filter elements combine strength and flexibility, making them versatile for various liquid and air filtration applications.</p></p>
