<p><p>The Injection Molding Grade High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Resin market is primarily categorized into two types: linear and non-linear. Linear HDPE is characterized by a high molecular weight and uniform structure, providing superior strength and rigidity, making it ideal for products requiring durability. Non-linear HDPE, on the other hand, exhibits a branched molecular structure, offering enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. These distinct types cater to diverse applications in packaging, automotive, and consumer goods, allowing manufacturers to choose based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
