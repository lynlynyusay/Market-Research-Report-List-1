<p><ul><li>Single Loop</li><li>Multiple Loops</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The analog thermostat market is segmented into two main types: single loop and multiple loops. Single loop thermostats control temperature for a single zone, ideal for simpler applications like residential heating or cooling systems. In contrast, multiple loop thermostats manage temperature across various zones or systems, suitable for larger or more complex environments, such as commercial buildings. Each type caters to specific needs, with single loop offering simplicity and multiple loops providing enhanced control and efficiency for diverse temperature management.</p></p>
