<p><p>The root canal irrigant market comprises several key types: Sodium Hypochlorite, EDTA, Chlorhexidine, and others. Chlorhexidine is favored for its antimicrobial efficacy and residual effect. Other irrigants may include herbal or biocompatible alternatives. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing endodontic treatment outcomes by ensuring thorough disinfection and preparation of the root canal system.</p></p>
