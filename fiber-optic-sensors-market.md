<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic sensors market is divided into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors use the optical fiber itself as the sensing element, where changes in light properties within the fiber indicate environmental changes. Extrinsic fiber-optic sensors, on the other hand, utilize external devices and components to measure parameters, with the fiber transmitting signals from the external sensors to a monitoring system. Both types are widely used in various applications, including structural monitoring, temperature sensing, and medical diagnostics.</p></p>
