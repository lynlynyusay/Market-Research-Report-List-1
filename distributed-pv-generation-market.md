<p><ul><li>Rooftop</li><li>Ground-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed PV generation can be categorized into rooftop and ground-mounted markets. Rooftop systems involve photovoltaic panels installed on residential or commercial buildings, offering limited space but quick installation and minimal land use. They benefit from net metering and often allow owners to lower electricity bills. Ground-mounted systems, conversely, are installed on open land, providing larger capacity and scalability for utility-scale projects. This market type is suitable for solar farms and allows for optimized orientation and spacing of panels for maximum sunlight exposure.</p></p>
