<p><p>The leisure and landscaping artificial turf market comprises three main types of materials: PP (Polypropylene), PE (Polyethylene), and Nylon artificial grass turf. PP turf is cost-effective and suitable for low-traffic areas, offering durability and UV resistance. PE turf mimics natural grass closely, providing a soft feel and is ideal for residential lawns and recreational spaces. Nylon turf is the most robust option, known for its resilience and ability to withstand heavy use, making it perfect for sports fields and high-traffic areas.</p></p>
