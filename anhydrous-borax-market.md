<p><p>The anhydrous borax market is primarily categorized into two types: burning borax and fusing hydrated borax. Burning borax involves heating hydrated borax to remove water, resulting in a powder used in metallurgy and ceramics. Fusing hydrated borax, on the other hand, involves melting hydrated borax to create a glassy substance, often used as a flux in metalworking and glass production. Both types cater to specific industrial applications, driven by their unique properties and processing methods.</p></p>
