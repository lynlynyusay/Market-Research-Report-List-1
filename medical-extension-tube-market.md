<p><p>The medical extension tube market includes one-way tubes, 2-way tubes, and multipath tubes, each serving distinct purposes in medical applications. One-way tubes facilitate unidirectional flow, ensuring safe delivery of fluids or gases. 2-way tubes enable bidirectional flow, allowing for both delivery and drainage. Multipath tubes are designed to support multiple connections, offering versatility in complex medical systems. These tubes are essential in various healthcare settings, optimizing patient care and enhancing the efficiency of fluid management practices.</p></p>
