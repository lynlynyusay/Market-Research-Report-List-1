<p><p>The gout disease treatment market is segmented into two primary types: acute gout and chronic gout. In contrast, chronic gout management aims for long-term control through urate-lowering therapies, such as allopurinol or febuxostat, and lifestyle modifications to prevent future attacks. These distinctions address the varying patient needs and treatment approaches within the gout management landscape.</p></p>
