<p><ul><li>Self Generating Lights</li><li>Battery Powered Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle taillight market is primarily divided into self-generating lights and battery-powered lights. Self-generating lights utilize kinetic energy from pedaling, offering an eco-friendly option that eliminates the need for batteries. These lights automatically charge while riding, ensuring consistent illumination. In contrast, battery-powered lights rely on replaceable or rechargeable batteries, providing flexibility in brightness and features but requiring periodic battery replacements. Both types aim to enhance visibility and safety for cyclists, catering to varying user preferences and riding conditions.</p></p>
