<p><p>Form Fill Seal (FFS) machines are essential in packaging operations, categorized mainly into vertical and horizontal markets. Vertical FFS machines operate with flexible films to create pouches from the top down, ideal for products like liquids and powders. In contrast, horizontal FFS machines form packages from a roll of film laid flat, suitable for solid items like snacks and pharmaceuticals. Each type caters to specific product dimensions and handling requirements, optimizing efficiency in various packaging environments.</p></p>
