<p><strong>The Optical Displacement Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSD Method</li><li>CMOS (CCD) Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Displacement Sensors market includes two primary methods: PSD (Position Sensitive Detector) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) or CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). PSD sensors rapidly detect position changes by measuring light intensity across their surface, enabling precise displacement measurements. In contrast, CMOS/CCD methods utilize image sensors to capture light patterns, allowing for high-resolution imaging and displacement detection. Both methods are critical in various applications, including industrial automation, robotics, and quality control, enhancing measurement accuracy and efficiency.</p></p>
